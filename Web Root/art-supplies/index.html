Art supplies

David Marshall March 24, 2010 (updated March 29, 2025)

Tools of the trade

Digital media may be the norm these days, but more and more artists are taking to ink-on-paper as a creative alternative. This list is updated often, and curated into essential and optional "nice to have" categories. Whether you're new to tradition media's inky madness, or are taking a refresher, here are the supplies you'll need to start creating your own comics!

Check out the comics-specific Joe Kubert art store
