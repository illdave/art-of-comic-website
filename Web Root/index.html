Make Comics with Traditional Media

Learn professional ink-on-paper techniques: storytelling, inking with brushes and dip pens, character design, and production workflows.

College-level workshop • Real-world assignments • Small classes

Who Takes This Workshop

Career Changers & Hobbyists

No drawing experience needed. Assignments structured for 6-8 hours per week outside class.

Aspiring Professionals

Build portfolio pieces for art school or industry work. Recent students accepted to MassArt, SCAD, and CCS.

What You'll Learn

Sequential storytelling

Transform scripts into layouts

Traditional inking

Master brushes, pens, line weight, spot blacks

Production skills

From thumbnails to print-ready artwork

Character design

Consistent characters across panels and pages

About the Instructor

David Marshall has taught at Massachusetts College of Art and Design since 2011. Published by Fantagraphics Books, his comics appear alongside professionals working for DC and Marvel.
