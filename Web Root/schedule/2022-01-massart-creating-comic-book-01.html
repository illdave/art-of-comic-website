LALW-415-01
  • Monday evenings • Remote instruction
  • Massachusetts College of Art and Design • Liberal Arts Department
  • January 24 - May 9, 2022

Course description

Session 1: Orientation & First Assignment

January 24

Class Activities
Demos: Tools (pens, brushes, Ames Lettering Guide), straight lines, thumbnails, pencilling 10×15" art area on 14×17" paper (video)
Studio: Tool practice, life drawing, start thumbnails for Slice of Life
Lecture: History (Windsor McCay, George Herriman, Hal Foster), Technology Triangle, essential elements (title, credits, page numbers)
Due Next Session
Email instructor (within 48 hours)
One page ink practice
One page inked straight lines (pen and brush)
Two blank bristol pages with 10×15" art area penciled for Slice of Life

Session 2: Story Development

January 31

Class Activities
Demos: Advanced lettering (tiles, sound effects, blurbs, word balloons), page bordering with ruling pen
Studio: Lettering practice, first story conferences for Slice of Life
Lecture: Storytelling (staging, camera angles, page design), thumbnailing, creative processes, history (Walt Kelly, Roy Crane, Will Eisner)
Due Next Session
2-4 pages lettering practice
1-2 pages straight lines with ink
Continue developing Slice of Life

Session 3: Story Development

February 7

Class Activities
Demos: Ink techniques (brush and pen), video tutorials
Studio: Second story conferences for Slice of Life
Lecture: Importance of solid-black inking, history (R. Crumb, Jason, Jaime Hernandez)
Due Next Session
2-4 pages lettering practice
1-2 pages straight lines with ink
Finish thumbnails, create 10×15" pages, finish penciling 3 pages for Slice of Life
Resources
Hand lettering: Lettering Comics by Hand, Ames Guide tutorial, video demo, word balloons, ink basics
Lettering history: Thought Balloons Roundtable, Comicraft Glossary

Session 4: Art Development

February 14

Class Activities
Studio: Syllabus change explanation, continue work on Slice of Life and practice sheets
Due Session 5 (Two Weeks)

2-4 pages lettering practice B
1-2 pages straight lines with ink
Finish original art for Slice of Life
