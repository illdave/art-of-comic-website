Schedule

Current information on tuition, credits and registration options. Art of the Comic Book is a beginner-level class designed for anyone who wants to learn about the process of creating comics, or is looking to build a portfolio of sequential artwork to apply for a longer cartooning course. The course is open to creative writers, graphic and fine artists, comics aficionados and individuals who would like to develop writing and illustration skills to create a graphic novel.
